CAS AIC triad game

We will play on the basis of AIC: availability, integrity and confidentiality

The Rules of the game

  1. You have to get to the next page. Find a way. Clicking on things is just one of the ways to get there
  2. You need to access 4 different pages
  3. Google is your friend, but not your only friend
  4. Feel like a geek sometimes, right-click on the page to view source. There might be hints
  5. Everything on the page is a clue, just don't expect direct answers
  6. Let's have fun!
