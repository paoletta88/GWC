Girls! Welcome to our game!

We will play on the basis of the CIA triad: Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability. The CIA triad is a simple but widely-applicable security model summarizing three key principles which should be guaranteed in any kind of secure system.
Confidentiality

The ability to hide information from those people unauthorised to view it, while making sure that the right people can in fact get it.
Integrity

The ability to ensure that data is an accurate and unchanged representation of the original secure information.
Availability

It is important to ensure that the information concerned is readily accessible to the authorised viewer at all times.
